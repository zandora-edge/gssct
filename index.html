About US

Gandhi Seva Sadan Charitable Trust is established by Sri Sadanam Kumaran andMrs. Sarojini Amma in 2005.The idea was to establish a center of excellence panIndia. Late Sadanam Kumaran was a freedom fighter who was jailed by the BritishRaj as a part of Quit India Movement in 1942. He escaped from the jail and wascaught by the British authorities while he was underground and put on fetters as apunishment under rigorous imprisonment. Sri Sadanam Kumaran came back to hisvillage, on call from Mahatma Gandhi to the youths to go back to the villages andstart reconstructing India from grassroots in order to be ready to take over fromtheBritishoncetheyleave.

He established an aided school for Harijans which had to be closed later due topoliticalinterventions.Heestablished3institutionsafterthat.

  • GandhiSevaSadan
  • Gandhi Seva Sadan Kathakali and Classic Art Academy
  • SevaSadanCentralSchool

All these three institutions are registered under societies Act of 1860 as separatesocieties.
